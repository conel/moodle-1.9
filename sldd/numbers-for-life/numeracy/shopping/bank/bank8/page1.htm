Introduction 8 (cont). Withdrawing cash

The easiest way for Sadia to withdraw cash is via the cash machine. She will need to:

• Put her card into the cash-point machine

• Enter her pin number
• Choose the option Cash Withdrawal
• Key in the amount she wants to withdraw
• Collect her card
• Collect her money and receipt
