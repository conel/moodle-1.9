Grading for the text selection question

In the text selection question the student is awarded a grade based on the words they select. Words if selected may be :

  • ignored
  • marked correct
  • or marked wrong

Students get a point for each word they correctly selected but loose a point for each words wrongly selected.

Percentage grade = ( total right - total wrong ) x 100 / total expected to select

The minimum grade is zero you can't get a negative grade.
