Movie

Instructions for authoring Flash movies that will work within this Flash Activity are available at http://jamiep.org

Most movies just need to be uploaded to the directory "mod/flash/movies/{moviename}/" within your Moodle site (where {moviename} is the name of your movie and you have a minimum of two files {moviename}.swf and {moviename}.html)

Movies that have been uploaded to this directory will be automatically detected and will then appear in the 'Movie' drop down list.
